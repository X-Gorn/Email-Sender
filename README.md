## Email-Sender (Bot)
---

Telegram RoBot to Sent Email via Telegram.

## Important Note:
* *Turn ON your Less Secure Apps from https://myaccount.google.com/lesssecureapps*

* Make sure it was Turned ON in your Sender Email, else bot won't working for you.
